FOOD AND DRINK SPOT


Here is the famous food and drinks restaurants at Kuala Kangsar that being a place for community in Kuala Kangsar eat, drinks and some of them this is their source of income.



Yut Loy Restaurant

No. Tel: 05-776 6369

51, Jalan Kangsar, Bandar Baru, 33000 Kuala Kangsar, Perak

In Kuala Kangsar, there is just one noteworthy dining establishment: Yut Loy (established 1939), a long-time favourite dining site of the Sultan of Perak, members of Perak aristocracy, and students from the elite boys' school, Malay College Kuala Kangsar (MCKK).

Laksa Lubuk

No. Tel : 05-776 8875

KPW Kubang Panjang, Kampung Paya, Kuala Kangsar, Perak

Fans of laksa can try something new here. Cooked with a wood stove, not only does it make this restaurant unique, but also evokes the taste and aroma of laksa which is also called as this wet laksa. Many customers agree that it is the best laksa in Kuala Kangsar. Coupled with cendol as a throat freshener after eating laksa, itâ€™s really a solid coupling.

Laksa Pokok Limau

No. Tel : 019-422 9512

195A, Jalan Besar Utara Selatan, Kampung Bendang Susur

Laksa Pokok Limau is quite famous for its nest eggs. His name is well known to the people of Perak because of his popularity. In fact, it once appeared in the show Jom Terokai Perak!

Warung Telaga

No. Tel : 012-593 4771

Batu 30, Kota Lama Kiri, Kuala Kangsar

Laksa sarang which is quite famous in Perak can also be found at Warung Telaga. With a beautiful view of the paddy fields, this stall was originally a place for field workers to unwind. The interior decoration of Warung Telaga emphasizes the classic Malay style that gives a sense of home. Apart from Laksa Sarang, customers also love boiled noodles, keropok lekor and cucur udang.

Chor Char Koay Teow Kuale

No. Tel : 019-492 4242

Kampung Penaga, 33000 Kuala Kangsar, Perak

Chor Char Koay Teow Kuale Restaurant not only serves char koay teow, but it also serves western cuisine. The price is very affordable, only RM3.50 for regular char koay teow and around RM15 for Western food. Customers who come here consider Chor Char Koay Teow Kuale serves the best char koay teow in Kuala Kangsar. Another favorite dish is grilled tofu

Burger Banjir River View

No. Tel : 013-472 6594

20, Jalan Istana, 33000 Kuala Kangsar, Perak

This restaurant serves up pretty much black pepper gravy on a plate along with your burgers and fries. The flood burger comes in two options of chicken and meat and both are priced at RM5 only while the chicken chop is sold at RM8.50. Looking at the price, quality and quantity of its abundant food, then it is not impossible that many people like to recommend this place.

Restoran Kak Bitah

No. Tel : 05-773 1006

1, Jalan Taiping, 33000 Kuala Kangsar, Perak

The restaurant's signboard states that they sell Malay cuisine, Tom Yam, Roti Canai and Seafood. According to many customers, they like to eat roti canai here the most. As the restaurant is open 24 hours a day, this place is a preferred location to hang out especially for those who feel hungry in the middle of the night. The price of food here is also cheap, making it another reason why it is the choice of many.

Teratak Warisan Kampung

No. Tel : 016-558 4955

Persiaran Bendahara 2, Bandar Baru, 33000 Kuala Kangsar, Perak

For the owner of Teratak Warisan Kampung located behind the Kuala Kangsar bus station, Perak, it is his responsibility to ensure that the ancestral heritage food is not lost to time. The foods on display are classified into several parts such as rice or glutinous rice, cakes, sweets and detergents. mouthwash, side dishes, vegetables or fruits and sambal or dipping.These foods are a popular traditional cuisine in earlier societies that are now threatened with extinction.
